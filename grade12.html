In grade 12 I have chosen several courses that are either required or will be needed for entry to my preferred university, Carleton University. Lastly, for the electives, I took Computer Engineering and Science because I really want to learn about the hardware and software of computers. This year, I should really work as hard as I can because I want to try to achieve a 95+ average. I wish to attain that average because I would get the most scholarship money that way as university costs a lot, if I want to pursue a Master's Degree it would probably cost even more.
